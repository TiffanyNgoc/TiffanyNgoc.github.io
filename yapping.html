First Merced Cars & Coffee

Check out my fresh take from the Merced Cars & Coffee meet! It was my first dive into car meet photography, and the experience was amazing. I was all about capturing the raw emotions and stories behind these machines and their proud owners. This post is packed with my favorite shots and the moments that made the day unforgettable. Dive in to see through my eyes – where every snapshot tells a part of the bigger story of car culture and community.
