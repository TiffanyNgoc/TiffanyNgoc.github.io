First Merced Cars & Coffee

Small, but Strong Community

I usually despise waking up early, like 8 AM early. This Cars and Coffee had a meetup at 8:40AM. Did I wake up in time? Most definitely. One of my passions is cars, and I risk being sleep deprived just to view a metal box with an engine in them. My day went like this: I woke up 30 min before the event started in my dorm. Making sure as to not wake up my roommates, I quickly got ready and made sure all of my gear was set. Such as making sure all my batteries were fully charged and my camera was fully prepped. I then left the dorm and quickly went to my car, I wanted to be one of the first people to get there to snap pics of cars entering. As I arrived at the location, there were already a couple cars parked. I parked a bit far as I didn't want my electic car to get in the way (I know, I also wished I had gas). I then took out my camera and prepared for the cars that would soon roll in.

This was my first actualy photography shoot, I had never really had any experience with the camera besides a couple class I took in school. So I was a total newbie and was learning. I set my settings to manual and adjusted the ISO, aperature, and shutter speed as needed.
